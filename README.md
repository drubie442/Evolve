# Specification #

Many people don’t know where to turn for mental health support or how to take the first step, especially when they are already overwhelmed or distressed. Despite operating the free, walk‑in Evolve Mental Health & Wellbeing Hub, LMNSPN is not consistently reaching people early enough. Supports are not always visible or easy to access, leading some individuals to delay seeking help or escalate to crisis, including repeated presentations to emergency departments (ED).

What success looks like: A scalable way to reach households with clear, accessible mental health information and simple pathways to support. People can connect easily through  digital or hybrid channels and are quickly guided to the right help. This leads to increased earl                                            y engagement with services like the Evolve Hub, More people access support earlier, feel connected, and navigate the mental health system with confidence.

# Dave's notes #

- Catalogue of wellbeing challenges across 7 categories: Everyday, Body, Mind, Social, Creative, Outdoors, Community
- Each challenge has: title, description, difficulty (Easy / Medium / Adventure), XP reward, and accessibility tag (any / seated / indoor / outdoor)
- Completing a challenge requires submitting a short free-text response (honour system; no answer checking)
- XP is added to the user's total; challenges can only be completed once per day

### Buddy Wall (`/buddy`) — authenticated to send, public to view
- Users pick from a set of **pre-written canned encouragement messages** and post them anonymously to a shared feed
- Feed auto-refreshes every 15 seconds
- No personal information is shared — messages are just attributed to a display name
- Sending encouragements earns **Encourager medals** (at 5 and 25 sent)
